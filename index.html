The Framework

5 Trust Signals We Audit

1
E-E-A-T Across Locations
Each location needs to reflect real expertise, not copy-paste content from head office. Google rewards demonstrable, location-specific authority.
2
Review Volume & Sentiment
Wildly inconsistent star ratings across locations signal quality issues that reduce brand trust in AI models and search engines alike.
3
NAP Consistency
Inconsistent names, addresses or phone numbers confuse the entity graph — meaning AI can't confidently surface your brand.
4
Third-Party Citations
Unlinked mentions in authoritative publications now function as trust signals. AI trains on the open web — your PR footprint matters.
5
Branded Search Volume
Low branded search is a proxy for low real-world reputation. Stable or growing branded queries signal a brand worth citing.
